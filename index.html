What is Dust and Mold?

Dust and Mold is the design consultancy of Montréal-based interactive designer Kevin Finlayson (nice to meet you!)

My goal in every project is to find a unique, beautifully-realized way to meaningfully communicate the message of your organization, using both tried and true methods and exciting new tools.

Services and expertise offered

I work in a variety of roles with distributed teams, or provide turn-key production incuding:

  • Graphic and interactive design for desktop and mobile
  • Front-end development including HTML5, CSS3, and javascript (jQuery primarily)
  • CMS implementation (Expression Engine, Wordpress, Perch)
  • E-Commerce service templating (Big Cartel, Shopify)
  • Server-side programming (PHP, Ruby)
  • Application development (Ruby on Rails)

I’m also experienced with information architecture design, prototyping, research, user testing and feedback, as well as overall project management.
