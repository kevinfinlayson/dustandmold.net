I’m passionate and opinionated about web design, and believe that…

    Clarity is key
    Understanding and communicating your message is my goal

    Your website should be unique, rather than simply follow styles and trends

  • 3

    Relationships matter
    Communication, collaboration, critical dialogue—all these are at the heart of any design success

  • 4

    Best practices always
    Semantic markup, hand-crafted code, scalable application design—we got those, and more

  • 5

    Mobile is the way forward
    I use a ‘mobile-first’ approach to make my websites easy to use on any platform

  • 6

    Great work takes time and effort
    I show up and work steadily, iterating towards the best possible solutions
